## Hey, I'm Graham

I build infrastructure and platforms that help engineers move faster.

I've worked across the industry from DevOps at [syapse](https://github.com/syapse) to building data platforms at [facetdata](https://github.com/facetdata) (acquired by [snap](https://github.com/snapchat/)), then BI tools at [rill](https://github.com/rilldata), and now at [degreed](https://www.degreed.com/) focusing on human-agent collaboration at scale. The common thread isn't the title, it's the same principle: find where things are slow or broken, and build something clean that just works.

When I'm not at work, I'm usually fixing old Volkswagens. Same philosophy applies—work within constraints, find elegant solutions, and strip away everything that doesn't matter. Whether it's operational overhead or a bosch CIS system, the goal is the same: make it simple.

I'm curious about distributed systems, infrastructure, containers, and how to build tools that engineers actually want to use. If you want to chat about any of that, feel free to reach out.
